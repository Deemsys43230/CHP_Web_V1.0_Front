Title : Obesity

Posted by : CyberHealths

Posted on : Mar 29,2017

Banner Image

 

Short Description

Overweight and obesity together make up one of the leading preventable causes of death in the U.S. More than a third of U.S.adults are obese. Approximately 17%, of U.S. children and adolescents ages 2 to 19 are obese.
