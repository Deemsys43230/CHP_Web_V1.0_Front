Global report on Diabetes

  • By Cyber Health

Diabetes is a chronic condition in which sugars build up in the blood beyond a normal level. Healthy body uses the hormone insulin to move sugars (“glucose”) into the cells. In diabetes body either cannot produce enough insulin or is resistant to its own insulin, so excess sugars can’t move into the cells and, instead, stay in the blood.
