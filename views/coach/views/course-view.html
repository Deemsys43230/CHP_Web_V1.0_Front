Natural Health Tips for Skin

Combination skin is usually dry or normal in some areas but oily in the T-zone – a fancy name for the forehead, nose, and chin area of your face. If this is your skin type, you might be struggling with enlarged pores, blackheads, and some grease and shine along your T-zone.
