Combination skin is usually dry or normal in some areas but oily in the T-zone – a fancy name for the forehead, nose, and chin area of your face. If this is your skin type, you might be struggling with enlarged pores, blackheads, and some grease and shine along your T-zone.

Dry Skin
Dry skin does not hold oil and moisture well. It feels tight and can become rough, itchy, and flaky. If this is your skin type, use a gentle cleanser and avoid washing your face too many times. Apply and re-apply a rich moisturiser throughout the day.

Oily Skin
Oily skin usually gets shiny or greasy pretty quickly. This is because this skin type produces extra oil, which can eventually clog pores, attract germs, and cause acne. If this is your skin type, fight the urge to pop or prick your pimples! Stick to skincare products that are oil-free and non-comedogenic, meaning they won’t clog pores.

Sensitive Skin
Sensitive skin is easily irritated, becoming itchy, red, painful, and dry. If this is your skin type, find out which factors trigger a reaction and avoid them. It’s better to confirm with your dermatologist which product from the CLEAN & CLEAR® range will suit you best.
